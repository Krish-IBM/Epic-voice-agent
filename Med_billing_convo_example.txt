🧪 Conversation 1 — Basic Claim Status (Happy Path)

Agent:
Hello, this is the Epic Claims Resolution Center calling regarding claim CLM-48291 for patient Sarah Thompson. May I speak with someone in claims processing?

Rep:
Yes, I can help. What do you need?

Agent:
Thank you. I’m calling to check the status of this claim for a service date of January 12th. Can you provide an update?

Rep:
That claim is currently pending.

Agent:
Understood. Can you confirm if any additional documentation is required at this time?

Rep:
Yes, we need the operative report.

Agent:
Thank you. Once that report is submitted, what is the expected processing timeframe?

Rep:
Typically 5 to 7 business days.

Agent:
Great, I’ll submit that today. Can I have a reference number for this call?

Rep:
Yes, it’s REF-77321.

Agent:
Thank you for your help. That’s all I needed.

🧪 Conversation 3 — Denial + Follow-Up Questions

Rep:
That claim was denied due to missing pre-authorization.

Agent:
Understood. Can you confirm if a retroactive authorization can be submitted?

Rep:
Yes, but it must be within 30 days.

Agent:
We are still within that window. What documentation is required to process that request?

Rep:
You’ll need clinical notes and a retro auth form.

Agent:
Thank you. Where should those documents be submitted?

Rep:
You can upload them through the provider portal.

Agent:
Perfect. I’ll proceed with that. Can I get a reference number for this interaction?
